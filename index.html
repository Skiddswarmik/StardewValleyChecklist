Critical path

This is the rough route. The season tabs have the actual checklist.

Do not sell these by accident

Keep one chest beside your house just for Community Center items.

  • Spring: 5 gold parsnips, green bean, cauliflower, potato, spring forage, rainy spring fish.
  • Summer: 5 gold melons, tomato, pepper, blueberry, melon, poppy, sunflower, wheat, fiddlehead fern, pufferfish, sturgeon.
  • Fall: 5 gold pumpkins, corn, eggplant, pumpkin, yam, fall forage, walleye, tiger trout, apples, pomegranate, truffle.
  • Winter: nautilus shell, winter forage, sturgeon or tuna if missed.
  • Mines: quartz, earth crystal, frozen tear, fire quartz, frozen geode, red mushroom, purple mushroom, solar essence, void essence.
  • Animals: large milk, large eggs, large goat milk, duck egg, duck feather, wool, rabbit's foot, truffle.

The items that usually ruin Year 1

Treat these like boss fights.

  • Red Cabbage: buy Red Cabbage or Red Cabbage Seeds from the Traveling Cart if possible.
  • Truffle: get a pig grown before winter, or buy from cart.
  • Rabbit's Foot: deluxe coop/rabbit, Skull Cavern serpent drop, or cart.
  • Duck Feather: duck friendship, or cart.
  • Fiddlehead Fern: Secret Woods in summer. Do not miss this.
  • Apples x3 + Pomegranate: fruit cave, fruit trees, or cart.
  • Sandfish: requires desert access unless bought from cart.
  • Walleye: fall rain only unless using a Rain Totem in winter.

Spring Checklist

Goal: open the Community Center, finish Spring crops/forage, hit early fish windows, start tappers, and push the mines.

Summer Checklist

Goal: get the risky summer-only items, build income, start fruit tree backup, and set up animals hard.

Fall Checklist

Goal: finish crop bundles, catch rain fish, harvest fruit tree items, secure truffle before winter, and repair the bus.

Winter Checklist

Goal: clean up winter forage, nautilus shell, missed winter fish, animal items, cooking, and final donations.

Year-Round / Setup Checklist

These can be handled across multiple seasons, but starting early makes Year 1 completion much easier.

Traveling Cart habit: check every Friday and Sunday in Cindersap Forest. Buy any Year 1 bottleneck item you see, even if it feels expensive.
